## What Affects Solar Panel Price? Key Cost Factors

Several factors influence the final price you pay for solar in Dhule:

### 1. Panel Technology
- **Mono PERC** (most common) — ₹24–30/W
- **TOPCon** (newer, higher efficiency) — ₹28–35/W
- **HJT** (premium, best heat performance) — ₹35–45/W

### 2. Inverter Brand and Type
- **String inverter** (standard) — ₹8,000–₹15,000 per kW
- **Micro-inverter** (panel-level) — ₹12,000–₹20,000 per kW
- **Hybrid inverter** (with battery port) — ₹15,000–₹25,000 per kW

### 3. Mounting Structure
- **RCC flat roof** (most Dhule homes) — standard cost
- **Tin/sheet roof** — slightly lower
- **Elevated structure** (if roof needs clearance) — 10–15% extra

### 4. System Size
Larger systems have a lower per-kW cost due to economies of scale. A 10 kW system costs ₹48,000–₹56,000 per kW, while a 1 kW system costs ₹55,000–₹65,000 per kW.

### 5. Installation Complexity
Multi-story buildings, shaded roofs requiring optimizers, or long cable runs can add 5–10% to the installation cost.

## Common Questions About Solar Panel Prices in Dhule

### Is solar really worth it in Dhule?

Absolutely. Dhule gets 5.0–5.2 sun hours per day — among the highest in Maharashtra. A 3 kW system pays for itself in under 3 years and then generates free electricity for the remaining 22+ years of its lifespan. With MSEDCL tariffs rising every year, your savings grow over time.

With the PM Surya Ghar subsidy, a 1 kW system costs as little as **₹25,000 after subsidy**. If you qualify for the SMART Solar scheme (BPL/SC/ST/EWS), you can get a 1 kW system for just **₹2,500–₹10,000**.

### Do solar panel prices include GST?

Yes, all prices listed above include GST. Solar panels attract 12% GST, but since solar systems are sold as a complete package (panels + inverter + mounting + installation), the effective GST works out lower. AceSolarTech provides a single all-inclusive quote with no hidden charges.

### How long do solar panels last?

Modern solar panels come with a **25-year performance warranty** guaranteeing at least 80% output at year 25. In practice, panels continue generating electricity for 30–35 years. Inverters typically last 10–15 years and may need replacement once during the panel lifetime.

### Will my roof support solar panels?

A 1 kW solar system weighs approximately 60–75 kg spread over 80 sq ft. Most RCC (concrete) rooftops in Dhule can easily support this weight. Our team conducts a **free site visit** to assess your roof structure, shading, and orientation before providing a final quote.

### What happens on cloudy or rainy days?

Solar panels still generate electricity on cloudy days — typically 25–40% of their peak output. During monsoon season (June–September), your generation will be lower, but the surplus credits earned during Dhule's hot, sunny months (October–May) more than compensate. Net metering lets you carry forward credits.
